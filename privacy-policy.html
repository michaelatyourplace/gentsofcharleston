IMPORTANT NOTICE REGARDING TEXT MESSAGING DATA

Gents of Charleston (“we,” “us,” or “our”) does NOT share customer opt-in information, including phone numbers and consent records, with any affiliates or third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. All SMS opt-in data is kept strictly confidential and used only to provide requested services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and physical address
  • Payment information when booking or requesting services
  • Opt-in records and timestamps for SMS and email communications

Non-Personal Information:

  • IP address, browser type, and device information
  • Website usage data and analytics
  • Cookies and tracking technologies

Customer Communication:

  • Service inquiries and booking details
  • Appointment preferences and event information
  • Feedback and service history

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process payments and bookings
  • Communicate regarding inquiries, appointments, and updates
  • Enhance website functionality and user experience
  • Maintain communication consent records
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

3. SMS Messaging & Compliance

By opting into SMS communications, you agree to receive messages related to bookings, reminders, and support.

Opt-In & Consent:

  • Messages are only sent with explicit consent
  • We maintain timestamped opt-in records
  • We comply with TCPA and applicable regulations

Opt-Out:

  • Reply “STOP” at any time to unsubscribe
  • You will receive confirmation and no further messages
  • Requests are processed within 24 hours

Message Details:

  • Frequency varies based on your interaction
  • Messages relate only to requested services
  • No promotional messages without consent

Support:

  • Reply “HELP” for assistance
  • Standard message/data rates may apply

SMS Data Protection: No mobile information will be shared with third parties for marketing purposes. Data may only be shared with service providers necessary to deliver messaging services.

4. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent personal information. Information may be shared with:

  • Service providers (payment processors, booking systems)

SMS opt-in data and consent will never be shared outside of required service providers.

5. Data Security

While we take strong precautions, no system is 100% secure. We use commercially reasonable measures to protect your information.
